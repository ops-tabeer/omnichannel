Customer Support Platform

Tabeer Tours operates a customer support platform powered by Chatwoot that enables businesses to manage customer conversations from Facebook Messenger efficiently.

What We Provide

Our platform offers a unified inbox solution for businesses to handle customer inquiries through Facebook Messenger. This service allows companies to:

  • Connect their Facebook Pages to manage customer messages
  • Respond to customer inquiries in real-time
  • Manage multiple conversations through a centralized dashboard
  • Provide efficient customer support across Facebook Messenger

How We Use Facebook Platform Data

When a business connects their Facebook Page to our platform, we access the following data solely for customer support purposes:

  • Page Information: To list and connect Facebook Pages that users manage
  • Messages: To receive and display customer messages in our support inbox
  • User Profile Data: Customer name and profile picture to help support agents identify who they're communicating with

All data is used exclusively within our application to facilitate customer support conversations. We do not share, sell, or use this data for any marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Privacy & Security

We are committed to protecting user data and maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security. All Facebook Platform Data is handled in accordance with Facebook's Platform Terms and our Privacy Policy.
